About Eskom

Eskom is South Africa’s leading electricity provider, established in 1923 with the mission to generate, transmit, and distribute electricity to homes, businesses, and industries across the nation. As a state-owned enterprise, we play a vital role in powering the economy and ensuring a stable supply of energy for millions of South Africans.

Our mission

At Eskom, our mission is to provide reliable, sustainable, and affordable electricity. We are committed to supporting economic growth and improving the quality of life for all South Africans through responsible energy management and innovation.

Eskom Distribution coverage

  • National Coverage: Eskom supplies electricity to a vast area of South Africa, serving both urban and rural communities.
  • Major Urban Centers: Key cities like Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban, and Pretoria are connected to Eskom’s distribution network, providing reliable electricity to large populations.
  • Rural Electrification: Eskom is dedicated to extending its services to remote and rural areas, working to improve access to electricity for underserved communities.

    • Our Partners

  • Municipal Distributors: In some areas, Eskom collaborates with municipal distributors to ensure seamless electricity supply. Municipalities may handle the last mile of distribution.
  • Community Engagement: Eskom actively engages with communities to understand their needs and improve service delivery.
